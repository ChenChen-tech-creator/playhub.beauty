Words of Wonders

Solve word puzzles and discover wonders of the world

Game Details

Words of Wonders combines word puzzles with a virtual tour of world landmarks. Form words from letters to solve puzzles and progress through different world-famous locations. This engaging word game enhances vocabulary while providing beautiful visuals of global wonders. With thousands of levels and increasing difficulty, Words of Wonders offers a perfect blend of education and entertainment that will challenge your word skills.

How to Play

  • Connect letters by swiping to form words
  • Find words that match the crossword puzzle layout
  • Discover all the words to complete each level
  • Unlock new world landmarks as you progress
  • Use hints when you're stuck on difficult words
  • Challenge yourself with increasingly complex word puzzles
