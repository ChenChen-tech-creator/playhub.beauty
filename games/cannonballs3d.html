Cannon Balls 3D

Fire cannon balls to destroy targets in this physics-based 3D game

Game Details

Cannon Balls 3D is an exciting physics-based game where you control a cannon to destroy various targets. With realistic physics and destructible environments, this game offers satisfying gameplay as you aim, fire, and watch the chaos unfold in beautiful 3D environments. Each level presents unique challenges and objectives, requiring strategic thinking and precise aim to progress.

How to Play

  • Aim your cannon by dragging to set angle and power
  • Release to fire at your targets
  • Hit targets efficiently to earn high scores
  • Use the environment and physics to your advantage
  • Complete level objectives to progress through the game
  • Adjust your strategy based on different target types and layouts
