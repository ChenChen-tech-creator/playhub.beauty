Zoo Boom

Manage your own virtual zoo in this exciting simulation game

Game Details

Zoo Boom lets you build and manage your own virtual zoo with colorful animals and exciting attractions. Plan your zoo layout, care for different animal species, and attract visitors to create the most successful zoo possible in this engaging simulation game. With charming graphics and intuitive controls, Zoo Boom provides hours of fun for animal lovers of all ages.

How to Play

  • Use touch or mouse controls to interact with the game elements
  • Place animal enclosures and build visitor amenities strategically
  • Manage your zoo's resources to ensure profitability
  • Keep your animals happy and well-cared for to attract more visitors
  • Complete challenges and objectives to unlock new animals and attractions
  • Expand your zoo as you earn more money and gain popularity
