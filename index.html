What is Smash Karts?

Ultimate Kart Racing Experience

Smash Karts is a free-to-play 3D multiplayer kart racing game that combines high-speed racing with strategic combat. Battle up to 24 players in real-time across colorful arenas filled with weapons and power-ups.

Created by Tall Team and launched in March 2020, Smash Karts has revolutionized browser-based racing games with its unique blend of Mario Kart-style gameplay and battle royale elements.

Smash Karts FAQ

Is Smash Karts free to play?

Yes! Smash Karts is completely free with no downloads required. All game modes, weapons, and core features are accessible without payment.

How many players can play together?

Smash Karts supports up to 24 players in a single match, creating intense multiplayer battles across various game modes.

What devices can run Smash Karts?

Smash Karts works on desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones through modern web browsers. No app download needed.

Are there different game modes?

Yes! Choose from Free For All, Capture The Flag, Gem Collector, Hat Holder, Team Battles, and more unique Smash Karts modes.

What are the system requirements?

Any device with a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and stable internet connection can run Smash Karts smoothly.

Can I customize my kart?

Absolutely! Unlock different kart designs, colors, and character customizations as you play and complete challenges in Smash Karts.

How do weapons work?

Drive into loot boxes to collect weapons. You can only carry one weapon at a time - use it before collecting another!

Are there regular updates?

Yes! Smash Karts receives seasonal updates with new arenas, themes, customization options, and gameplay features.
